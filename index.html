  • Generate Cartoon Faces using StyleGan

    I fine-tuned StyleGAN to generate high-quality cartoon faces. Using a custom dataset, I adapted the model to produce diverse cartoon images with distinct features. Moreover, I modified the original StyleGAN architecture to incorporate conditional inputs, allowing precise control over the generated faces. This enhancement enables the generation of cartoon faces with specific attributes, such as gender (male or female), age (young or old), and skin tone. These customizations were achieved by manipulating the latent space and refining the model to respond to these conditions, ensuring the output meets the desired criteria.

    Indeed, I modified facial emotions in generated images by manipulating the latent space directions of StyleGAN. I explored specific vectors in the latent space that correspond to various emotions like happiness, sadness, anger, and surprise. By adjusting the direction of these vectors, I was able to seamlessly change the emotional expression of generated faces while maintaining their core identity and other attributes, such as gender and age. This approach allows for precise and dynamic control over facial emotions in generated images, demonstrating the power of StyleGAN's latent space manipulation.


    Technologies: Python, Pytorch, StyleGAN2, Google Colab, Google Cloud Platform.

  • Face Recognition

    I developed a workflow to automate the process of extracting and enhancing cartoon faces from video files. The workflow begins by downloading cartoon movies, followed by detecting all faces in the video frames. Additionally, I integrated a super-resolution model to upscale and enhance the quality of the extracted faces, ensuring high-resolution output. This end-to-end pipeline efficiently processes cartoon movies, resulting in high-quality, precisely cropped facial images suitable for further applications.


    Technologies: Python, Google Cloud Platform, Super Resolution Model.

  • Face Transformation using Semantic StyleGAN

    I employed Semantic StyleGAN to perform targeted facial transformations by modifying specific facial segments. Leveraging semantic manipulation in the latent space, I was able to alter individual features such as hair style, hair color, and add facial hair (e.g., beards or mustaches) without affecting other aspects of the face. By isolating and adjusting these distinct facial regions, the transformations maintained a natural and cohesive appearance. This approach enables high flexibility in customizing specific facial attributes while preserving the overall identity of the generated face.


    Technologies: Python, Pytorch, Semantic StyleGAN, Google Colab, Google Cloud Platform.
